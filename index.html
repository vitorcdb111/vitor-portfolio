ABOUT

✉ in ★

Hi, I’m a Game Designer with a technical background in programming. I bring nearly seven years of experience in the tech industry, with the last four dedicated to game development. I strive to combine design and technology to create engaging and intuitive gameplay.

During my years at Oak3 Game Studio, I’ve had the opportunity to work on more than 25 projects, each challenging my skills in different ways.

I’ve contributed to educational games, AI and VR experiences, mobile and PC titles, Steam projects, and even unusual challenges such as developing mechanics for the Telegram platform.

My experience is centered around Unity and Unreal, combining technical thinking with a game designer’s perspective. I’ve also worked with various other technologies over the years, including Godot, web and mobile games, as well as AI and VR projects, consistently moving between design and development to create clear and functional experiences.

And I’m grateful to have collaborated with partners who trusted my work and helped shape my growth.
